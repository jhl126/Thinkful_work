General Communication, Inc., through its subsidiaries, provides a range of wireless, data, video, voice, and managed services to residential customers, businesses, governmental entities, and educational and medical institutions primarily in Alaska under the GCI brand. It offers Internet and local access services. As of December 31, 2016, the company had 222,500 wireless subscribers; 140,800 cable modem subscribers; and 125,800 basic video subscribers. General Communication, Inc. was founded in 1979 and is based in Anchorage, Alaska.
