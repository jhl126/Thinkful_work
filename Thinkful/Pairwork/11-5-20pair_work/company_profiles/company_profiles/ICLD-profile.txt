InterCloud Systems, Inc. provides networking orchestration and automation, for the Internet of things, software-defined networking (SDN), and network function virtualization (NFV) environments to telecommunications service provider and corporate enterprise markets in the United States and internationally. It operates in three segments: Applications and Infrastructure, Professional Services, and Managed Services. The company offers integrated cloud-based managed solutions that allow organizations to migrate and integrate their applications into a public, private, or hybrid cloud environment; hardware solutions and applications, cloud-based managed solutions, and professional staffing services; and vendor-independent and multi-vendor virtual network function validation services. It also provides applications and services, including SDN training, SDN software development and integration, vertical network function validation, unified communications, interactive voice response, and SIP-based call centers; and cabling and other field installations, as well as designs, engineers, installs, and maintains Wi-Fi and wide-area networks, DAS networks, and small cell distribution networks for local exchange carriers, telecommunications original equipment manufacturers (OEMs), cable broadband multiple system operators, and enterprise customers. In addition, the company designs, installs, and maintains hardware solutions for the OEMs that support voice, data, and optical networks. Further, it offers applications for managed data, converged services, voice recognition, session initiation protocol voice over IP, streaming media and unified communication collocation services, and others; and consulting solutions to the service-provider and enterprise markets, including project management, network implementation, network installation, network upgrades, rebuilds, maintenance, and consulting services. InterCloud Systems, Inc. was founded in 2006 and is based in Shrewsbury, New Jersey.
