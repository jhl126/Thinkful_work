Buffalo Wild Wings, Inc. owns, operates, and franchises restaurants under Buffalo Wild Wings, R Taco, and PizzaRev names. The company's restaurants provide various food products, as well as non-alcoholic and alcoholic beverages. As of February 7, 2017, it had approximately 1,220 Buffalo Wild Wings locations worldwide. The company was founded in 1982 and is headquartered in Minneapolis, Minnesota. As of February 5, 2018, Buffalo Wild Wings, Inc. operates as a subsidiary of Arby's Restaurant Group, Inc.
