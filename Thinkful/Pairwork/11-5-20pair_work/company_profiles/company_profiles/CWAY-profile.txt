Coastway Bancorp, Inc. operates as the bank holding company for Coastway Community Bank that provides various financial services to individuals, families, and businesses in Rhode Island. The company's deposit products include certificate of deposit, savings, non-interest-bearing demand deposit, money market, and club accounts. Its loan products comprise one- to four-family residential real estate loans, home equity loans and lines of credit, commercial real estate loans, U.S. small business administration loans, commercial business loans, commercial construction loans, and consumer loans. The company also offers online and mobile banking services. It operates nine banking offices located in Providence County and Kent County, Rhode Island. The company was founded in 1920 and is headquartered in Warwick, Rhode Island.
