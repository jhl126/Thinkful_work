CCUR Holdings, Inc. does not have significant business operations. The company intends to identify opportunities to invest in or acquire one or more operating businesses. Previously, it was engaged in the content delivery and storage business. The company was formerly known as Concurrent Computer Corporation and changed its name to CCUR Holdings, Inc. in January 2018. CCUR Holdings, Inc. was founded in 1966 and is headquartered in Duluth, Georgia.
