Shutterfly, Inc. manufactures and retails personalized products and services primarily in the United States, Canada, and the European Community. The company operates through Consumer and Shutterfly Business Solutions segments. It offers a range of personalized photo-based products and services that enable consumers to upload, edit, enhance, organize, find, share, create, print, and preserve their memories. The company also provides photo-based products, such as stationery, photo books, personalized gifts, photo prints, and photo-based merchandise services. In addition, it provides cards, personalized holiday cards, announcements, invitations and stationery products, and home decor products and photo gifts, as well as online photo services under the Shutterfly brand. Further, the company offers BorrowLenses, an online marketplace for photographic and video equipment rentals; and Groovebook, a mobile photo book app subscription service that prints mobile phone photos. Additionally, it provides personalized calendars, mugs, ornaments, candles, pillows, and blankets; and personalized direct marketing and other end-consumer communications services. The company was founded in 1999 and is headquartered in Redwood City, California.
