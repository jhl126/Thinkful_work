American Shared Hospital Services, together with its subsidiaries, leases radiosurgery and radiation therapy equipment to health care providers primarily in the United States. It offers radiosurgery equipment for the Gamma Knife stereotactic radiosurgery, a non-invasive procedure for the treatment of malignant and benign brain tumors, and arteriovenous malformations, as well as functional disorders, such as trigeminal neuralgia. The company also provides image guided radiation therapy systems, which integrate imaging and detection components into an accelerator, which enables clinicians to plan treatment, verify positioning, and deliver treatment. In addition, it offers financing services for Leksell Gamma Knife units. Further, the company provides proton beam radiation therapy and related equipment in Long Beach, California and Orlando, Florida, as well as offers planning, installation, reimbursement, and marketing support services to its customers. As of March 1, 2017, it operated 17 Gamma Knife units, as well as served 18 medical centers in 16 states of the United States. American Shared Hospital Services was founded in 1980 and is based in San Francisco, California.
