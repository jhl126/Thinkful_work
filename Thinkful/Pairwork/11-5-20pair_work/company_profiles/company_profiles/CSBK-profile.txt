Clifton Bancorp Inc. operates as the holding company for Clifton Savings Bank that provides various financial services to consumers and businesses in New Jersey. It offers liquid and term deposit instruments comprising checking, business checking, high-yield checking, passbook and statement savings, and club accounts, as well as certificates of deposit. The company also provides various loans, such as owner-occupied and one-to four-family mortgage loans; multi-family and commercial real estate loans; residential construction loans; commercial lines of credit; and consumer loans, including second mortgage loans and home equity lines of credit, as well as loans secured by passbook accounts and certificates of deposit. As of May 10, 2017, it offered its services through 12 full-service banking centers. Clifton Bancorp Inc. was founded in 1928 and is headquartered in Clifton, New Jersey.
