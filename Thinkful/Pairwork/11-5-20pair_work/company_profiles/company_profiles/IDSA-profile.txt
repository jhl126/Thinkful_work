Industrial Services of America, Inc. buys, processes, and markets ferrous and non-ferrous metals and other recyclable commodities. The company purchases ferrous and non-ferrous scrap metal primarily from industrial and commercial generators of steel, iron, aluminum, copper, stainless steel, and other metals, as well as from scrap dealers and retail customers; and processes scrap metal through its sorting, cutting, and baling operations. It sells scrap metal to steel mini-mills, integrated steel makers, foundries, and refineries. It also buys and sells used auto parts. The company was founded in 1953 and is based in Louisville, Kentucky.
