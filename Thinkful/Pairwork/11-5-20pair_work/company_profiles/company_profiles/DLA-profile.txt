Delta Apparel, Inc., through its subsidiaries, designs, manufactures, sources, and markets a portfolio of lifestyle basics and branded active wear apparel, headwear, and related accessory products in the United States and internationally. The company operates through two segments, Branded and Basics. It provides various casual and athletic products for men, women, juniors, youth, and children through various distribution channels and distribution tiers, including department stores, sporting goods and outdoor retailers, specialty stores, boutiques, mid and mass channels, e-retailers, and the U.S. military. The company markets fashion apparel garments, headwear, and accessories under the Salt Life, Soffe, and COAST brands, as well as other labels; and basic apparel garments under the Delta brand. It also manufactures private label products for branded sportswear companies, trendy regional brands, retailers, and sports licensed apparel marketers; and produces various types of on-demand digitally printed garments. In addition, the company sells its products direct-to-consumer on its Websites, as well as through its retail stores. Delta Apparel, Inc. was founded in 1999 and is headquartered in Greenville, South Carolina.
