Giga-tronics Incorporated, together with its subsidiaries, designs, manufactures, and markets a range of test and measurement equipment used in the development, test, and maintenance of wireless communications products and systems, flight navigational equipment, electronic defense systems, and automatic testing systems. The company operates in two segments, Giga-tronics Division and Microsource. The Giga-tronics Division segment produces modular signal sources, up-converters, receivers, and down-converters; and AXIe chassis and AXIe frequency reference modules for use with its signal sources. This segment offers products that are used primarily for the design, production, repair, and maintenance of products in aerospace, telecommunications, RADAR, and electronic warfare. The Microsource segment develops and manufactures a line of yttrium, iron, and garnet tuned oscillators, filters, and microwave synthesizers for use in operational applications, as well as in manufacturing a range of microwave instruments and devices. The company markets its products to commercial and government customers in the Americas, Europe, Asia, and internationally. Giga-tronics Incorporated was founded in 1980 and is based in Dublin, California.
