Westmoreland Resource Partners, LP, through its subsidiaries, produces and markets thermal coal in the United States. The company also produces surface mined coal. It operates 1 surface mine in Wyoming; and 4 active mining complexes in Ohio comprising 10 surface mines. The company markets its coal to large electric utilities with coal-fired power plants under coal sales contracts; and electric cooperatives, municipalities, and industrial customers in Indiana, Wyoming, Kentucky, Michigan, Ohio, Pennsylvania, and West Virginia. Westmoreland Resources GP, LLC operates as the general partner of the company. The company was formerly known as Oxford Resource Partners, LP and changed its name to Westmoreland Resource Partners, LP in January 2015. Westmoreland Resource Partners, LP was founded in 1985 and is based in Englewood, Colorado.
