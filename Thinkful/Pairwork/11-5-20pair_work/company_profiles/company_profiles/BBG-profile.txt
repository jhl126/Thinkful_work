Bill Barrett Corporation, an independent energy company, acquires, explores for, and develops oil and natural gas resources in the United States. The company primarily holds interests in the Denver-Julesburg basin located in the eastern plains of Colorado and parts of southeastern Wyoming. As of December 31, 2017, it had 245.2 net producing wells covering an area of 30,858 net undeveloped and 38,702 net developed acres with estimated proved reserves of 85.8 million barrels of oil equivalent. The company was founded in 2002 and is headquartered in Denver, Colorado.
