U.S. Energy Corp., an independent energy company, focuses on the acquisition and development of oil and gas producing properties in the continental United States. It holds interests in various oil and gas projects in Williston Basin of North Dakota; Zavala County and Dimmit County of Texas; and Coastal Louisiana. The company has estimated proved reserves of 887,142 barrels of oil equivalent. As of March 27, 2017, it had 95,839 gross acres of oil and gas leases; and 151 gross producing wells. The company was founded in 1966 and is based in Denver, Colorado.
