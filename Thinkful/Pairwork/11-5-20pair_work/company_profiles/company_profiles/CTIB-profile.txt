CTI Industries Corporation develops, produces, and sells consumer and film products for commercial and industrial applications worldwide. The company offers novelty products, including foil balloons, such as Superloons, Ultraloons, Miniloon, Card-B-Loons, Shape-A-Loons, and mini shapes; latex balloons primarily under the Partyloons name; toy balloon products, which include punch balls, water bombs, and Animal Twisties; Candy Blossoms that are containers with candy items and air-inflated balloons; and other inflatable toy items. It also provides vacuum sealable bags and rolls of film for household storage under the Ziploc and Zipvac brand names; valved and resealable bags for vacuum storage use; and vacuum sealing machines under the brand name of Ziploc. In addition, the company offers packaging films and custom film products for food, commercial, and packaging applications; and produces and distributes home organization and container products. It primarily serves various retail outlets, including general merchandise stores, discount and drugstore chains, grocery chains, card and gift shops, and party goods stores, as well as florists and balloon decorators. The company sells its products directly, as well as through a network of distributors and wholesalers, and independent sales representatives. CTI Industries Corporation was founded in 1975 and is headquartered in Lake Barrington, Illinois.
