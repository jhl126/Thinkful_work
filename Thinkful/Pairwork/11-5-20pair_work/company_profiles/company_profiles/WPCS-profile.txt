WPCS International Incorporated provides low voltage communication infrastructure services in the public services, healthcare, energy, education, and corporate enterprise markets primarily in the United States. It engages in the installation and service of voice and data networks, security systems, audio-visual solutions, and distributed antenna systems, as well as provision of and project management services. The company creates integrated building systems, including the installation of structured cabling systems; and develops networks for customer's wireless communication requirements comprising Wi-Fi networks, point-to-point systems, cellular networks, in-building systems, and two-way communication systems, as well as integrates telecommunications, life safety, environmental controls, security, and HVAC systems. The company was formerly known as Internet International Communications Ltd. and changed its name to WPCS International Incorporated in December 2004. WPCS International Incorporated was founded in 1997 and is headquartered in Suisun City, California.
