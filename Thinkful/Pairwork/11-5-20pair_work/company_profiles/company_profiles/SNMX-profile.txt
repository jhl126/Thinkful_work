Senomyx, Inc. discovers, develops, and commercializes flavor ingredients and natural high intensity sweeteners primarily for the packaged food, beverage, and ingredient supply industries. It develops and/or commercializes sweet, savory and salt flavor ingredients, bitter blockers and cooling agents. The company licenses its flavor ingredients to its collaborators on an exclusive or co-exclusive basis, as well as sells various flavor ingredients directly to flavor companies.
