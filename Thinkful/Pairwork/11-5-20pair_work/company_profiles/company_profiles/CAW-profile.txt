CCA Industries, Inc. manufactures and distributes health and beauty aid products in the United States, and internationally. It offers oral health-care products under the Plus+White brand; skin-care products under the Sudden Change and Porcelana brands; nail treatments under the Nutra Nail brand; pre and after-shave products under the Bikini Zone brand; depilatories under the Hair Off brand; sun-care products under the Solar Sense brand; perfumes under the Sunset Cafe brand; ear-care products under the Lobe Miracle brand; scar diminishing cream under the Scar Zone brand; and topical analgesic products under the Pain Bust*R II brand. The company markets its products to drug, food, and mass-merchandise retail chains; warehouse clubs; and wholesalers through sales representatives and distributors, as well as through Internet. CCA Industries, Inc. was founded in 1983 and is based in Lyndhurst, New Jersey.
