Solitario Zinc Corp., a zinc focused exploration company, engages in the acquisition, exploration, and development of zinc properties in North and South America. The company has joint venture interests in the Florida Canyon Zinc Project in Peru; and the Lik Zinc Project in Alaska. In addition, it has an additional portfolio of exploration properties and royalties in the Americas. The company was formerly known as Solitario Exploration & Royalty Corp. and changed its name to Solitario Zinc Corp. in July 2017. Solitario Zinc Corp. was founded in 1984 and is based in Wheat Ridge, Colorado.
